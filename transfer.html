EMU Transfer Student Posters


In this project, I was told to create a series of informational flyers promoting several welcoming events for EMU transfer students. Consistency was very important to me for this project, since the transfer students were likely to view each flier. background imagery was another important choice to me as I wanted to offer a welcoming tone and for each image to relate to the event that day.
