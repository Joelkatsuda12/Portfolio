Weekly Logos


For this assignment, I had to come up with 1 logo per week for a made-up company and have it mocked up. This was difficult for me because there was no brand/style guide to work off of, so it made me think about color/shape theory more as well as who the potential target audience would be. I really wanted to emphasize the tone of each logo and think about what emotions and tones audiences would feel. To come up with each company, I used a random company name generator that would give me a name and industry to work off of.
