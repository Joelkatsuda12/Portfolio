Train Schedule


For this project, I was asked to take the information from a regular train schedule, and redesign it to make it easier to read. The original train schedule that was given to me was heavily reliant on rows and columns of text, so having visual guides in this piece was something I really wanted to include in order to better visualize departures and arrivals. professors in college taught me that making information more easily accessible is helpful for everyone, and this was the first time that I really understood that concept.
