Album Cover Animation


For this project, I needed to come up with 2 pieces of design media for a mock client to simulate freelance design work. I chose the music artist “The Weeknd” since I am a very active listener and I understood what the tone of his music was. I chose an album cover and an animation because I wanted to sharpen my Photoshop and After Effects skills, while also capturing the mood of his music.
