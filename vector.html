Vector Art


These 9 pieces of vector art are personal projects that I created to learn more about Adobe Illustrator. Since I was attempting to learn the program myself, I took the same idea of drawing with vector art and applied it to create vector art of different mediums. Some of these pieces are sketches of other media like photos or album covers while others had no source material be used.
