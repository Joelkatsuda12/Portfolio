Fall Rush


To create this project, I drew each individual frame in Adobe Illustrator, then took each frame into Adobe Photoshop and animated them by using the animation tool. This project advanced my understanding of animation; I learned more about FPS and how it relates to stop motion, and I needed to find the exact FPS to give it a stop motion feel while moving fluidly at the same time. The inspiration was taken from animations of old baseball cartoons.
